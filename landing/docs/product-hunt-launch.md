## FAQ for Product Hunt Comments

**Q: How is this different from ChatGPT?**
A: ChatGPT chats, SecureAgent acts. We schedule reminders, control devices, post to social media, and make calls - not just talk about doing them.

**Q: Is it free?**
A: Yes! Free tier includes 100 AI requests/month. Pro plans start at $9/month.

**Q: What AI models do you use?**
A: We support 25+ models including GPT-4, Claude, Llama 3.3, Gemini, Mistral, and more. You choose which one to use.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Enterprise-grade security with AES-256 encryption, OWASP compliance, and HIPAA-ready infrastructure.

**Q: Can I self-host?**
A: Yes! Open-source version available on GitHub for self-hosting.
