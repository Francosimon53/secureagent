### Quick Start (2 minutes)

**Step 1: Connect a channel**

Choose where you want to use SecureAgent:

- [Connect Telegram](https://secureagent.vercel.app/dashboard/integrations) (most popular)
- [Connect Discord](https://secureagent.vercel.app/dashboard/integrations)

Here are some things you can try right now:

```
"Browse to CNN and summarize today's top 3 headlines"

"Set a reminder for tomorrow at 9am to check my emails"

"What's the weather in New York?"

"Create a daily task to send me a morning briefing"
```

This is where SecureAgent really shines. Go to [Dashboard → Scheduled Tasks](https://secureagent.vercel.app/dashboard/scheduler) and create your first automation.

Popular automations:
- Daily news digest at 8am
- Weekly summary of your calendar
- Daily weather forecast
- Price monitoring for products
